Marist Collage is a secondary school for boys in Athlone, County Westmeath, Ireland.

The school was founded in 1884 by the Marist Brothers, a French Order, and the first principle was Brother Mungo. The original scshool was based in Gleeson Street/ Saint Mary's Square. The collage had a seminary for Juniorate training between 1915 and 1936 located at Bailieborough Castle, County Caven

In 1973, the school moved its current location on Retreet Road. The current principle is Michael Dermody and the deputy principle is Paul Kelly.
